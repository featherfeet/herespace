~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~Concepts~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
klass -- physical map of the desks in a classroom with students' names attached to each desk -- corresponds to a specific group of students who go to a specific teacher's room at a specific time of day
assignment -- anything that students are supposed to do
    -- can be used for attendance
    -- can be used for classwork
    -- can be used for homework
    -- has a point value -- can be extra credit
participation -- teacher can add infractions/merits to student that aren't attached to a specific assignment
score -- ONE student's score on a particular assignment
    -- attached to a specific student and assignment
student -- a specific student
    -- has a name
    -- can be in multiple klasses
